About Us

eCheckID provides a mobile product information and marketing communication platform for brands to communicate directly with consumers. Our solution allows brands to uniquely identify each unit of product via the proprietary eCheckID code. This barcode, when scanned by the consumers’ mobile device, validates the authenticity of the product they purchase as well as provide safety information like allergy warning, product recall and usage information. Consumers can register their purchase of the product and interact with their social networks, all from the convenient of their mobile phone or tablet. The eCheckID app is also the repository of all discount and promotional offers from all the brands consumers care about. They can easily take advantage of loyalty, rebate or other promotional programs without having to do anything more than scanning the eCheckID code with their mobile device.


For brands, eCheckID’s cloud-based service delivers product information and marketing communication directly to targeted consumers. The cloud-based service can also administer loyalty, rebate or other promotional programs. Analytics based on the data from scans can provide actionable information relating to the sale, consumption, promotion and distribution of products.

eCheckID is offered as a complete end-to-end solution including:

  • Software for the creation of the encrypted eCheckID unique product identifier


  • Cloud-based service for consumer sign-up, code decryption and authentication, brand-consumer communication, and data management and analysis and


  • Downloadable mobile app acting as user-interface for product authentication and brand-consumer interaction.

eCheckID is headquartered in California’s Silicon Valley, and operates development and data center in Beijing and Chicago.
