for Consumers

With the proliferation of counterfeit consumer and health sensitive products worldwide, consumers are rightly concerned about the authenticity and integrity of the products they consume. For consumers, not knowing the integrity of branded products or the presence of allergens severely impairs user experience, and the chance for repeat purchases and referrals. Consumers need an easy way to check the authenticity, source, expiry, allergens, or recall status of products. Legacy methods of assuring product authenticity, like affixing holographic stickers to product packaging, are not reliable and cannot overcome fraudulent activities like obscuring expiration date or repackaging of recalled products. The complexity of today's distribution/resale channel also impacts customer experience as product information and marketing messages cannot be sent directly from the brand to the consumer in a timely, efficient manner, especially at the time of consumption.



A simple scan of the eCheckID code―which uniquely identifies each unit of product―assures the consumer that the product they purchase is genuine and safe. Safety recalls can be timely delivered to affected consumers’ cell phones with precise information as to what to do while not causing undue alarm. Consumers can indicate what they are allergic to (example, lactose, gluten, tree nuts, etc.) during app setup and they can be warned of the presence of these allergens in food items they purchase through a simple scan of the eCheckID code.



eCheckID can further enhance consumer experience by enabling direct interaction between the brand the consumers. Consumers can easily register their purchase or usage of the product, and based on the registration information, they can take advantage of promotional offers (if any) including loyalty program, rebate, discount for complementary products, or lottery. There is no need for consumers to log on to the brand’s web site to fill out a web form, or mail back registration card, sales receipt or part of the packaging in order to benefit from theses marketing programs.



The eCheckID app is also integrated with social networks like Facebook and Twitter. This allows consumers to interact with their social networks to post pictures of product usage, “Likes”, or exchange and comments about the product. For additional convenience, all promotion offers or product-related messages for all brands that support eCheckID are stored in a central location in the eCheckID app. Consumers no longer have to tediously search email boxes, chats, Facebook timeline, Tweets, text messages, or various web sites for critical product announcements or attractive promotional offers.

for Brands

eCheckID provides an easy to deploy service for brands to improve their brand equity by making their products harder to counterfeit . A simple scan of the eCheckID code allows brands to interact directly with consumers on their ubiquitous mobile phone or tablet. Critical product safety information, and cross-sell, up-sell or discount offers can be sent directly to targeted consumers. Loyalty and rebate programs can also be delivered to consumers using the eCheckID app. Brand owners can obtain critical usage and registration information about the consumers, as well as analytics about the efficacy of promotional offers, location and density of counterfeit products, or the diversion of products from authorized channel of distribution.



The benefits offered by eCheckID are enabled by the eCheckID code which uniquely identifies each unit of the product. This barcode is created from an encrypted sequence of alphanumeric characters that cannot be duplicated by counterfeiters. Once scanned by eCheckID app on the consumers’ mobile device, the barcode is translated and decrypted by the eCheckID cloud service. In response, product authenticity and other information related to the unit of product identified by the barcode are sent to the consumers’ mobile device. Consumers will also be warned if the scanned barcode fails to be authenticated, or if the product contains allergens identified by the consumers when they download the eCheckID app. If the consumer chooses to register their ownership of the product, key registration information will also be recorded. eCheckID also analyzes data collected to deliver actionable information about the pattern and location of consumption, location and density of counterfeit products, and diversion of products from legitimate channels.



The eCheckID solution is delivered as a cloud-service so it is very easy to deploy. In addition, the eCheckID barcode that uniquely identifies each unit of product can be designed with embedded logo and color scheme that enhance the brand identity of the product.
