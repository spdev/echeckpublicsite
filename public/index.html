for Consumers

eCheckID mobile product information platform enables consumers to consume with confidence by providing real-time information on authenticity, expiry, allergens, and/or recall status of health sensitive or other consumer packaged products. A simple scan of the eCheckID 2D barcode on the product packaging using the eCheckID app offers consumers immediate access to authenticity and safety information of each unit of product they purchase or consume. Consumers do not have to enter a complex string of alphanumerical characters onto the brands’ web site to determine authenticity, or try to check the holographic sticker or the printing on the packaging to guess the authenticity of a particular unit.


Unlike regular UPC or other marketing oriented 2D bar code, the eCheckID code contains encrypted, hard to counterfeit product identification information that identifies each specific unit of the product. Once scanned, critical up to date information―like safety recall―specific to the unit of product in the consumer’s possession is delivered instantaneously to the consumer’s mobile device. Consumer with certain allergic condition can be warned of the presence of allergens in the product like lactose, tree nuts, glutens, shell fish, etc.


Additional written product information, video or sound recording of usage ideas like recipe, usage direction, or comments from other customers or experts can be delivered directly to the consumer's smart phone or tablet to enhance consumer's appreciation of the product's benefits.


for Brands

eCheckID provides an easy-to-deploy service for brands to improve their brand equity by making their products harder to counterfeit and giving them a conduit for real-time consumer interaction. Critical product safety information, cross-sell, up-sell or discount offers can be sent directly to targeted consumers. Loyalty and rebate programs can also be implemented using the eCheckID app. Brand owners can obtain critical usage and registration information about the consumers, as well as analytics about the efficacy of promotional offers, location and density of counterfeit products, or the diversion of products from authorized channel of distribution.

These services are enabled through eCheckID’s cloud service and individualized product tracking using a unique label created from an encrypted digital signature that cannot be duplicated by counterfeiters. Once scanned by the eCheckID app on the consumers’ mobile device, the barcode is translated and decrypted by the eCheckID cloud service. In response, product authenticity and other information related to the unit of product are sent to the consumers’ mobile device. If the consumer chooses to register their ownership of the product, key registration information will be recorded in eCheckid’s central cloud service. eCheckID also analyzes data collected to deliver actionable information about the pattern and location of consumption, location and density of counterfeit products, and diversion of products from legitimate channels.

The eCheckID solution is delivered as a cloud-service so it is very easy to deploy. In addition, the eCheckID barcode that uniquely identifies each unit of product can be designed with embedded logo and color scheme that enhances the brand identity of the product.
